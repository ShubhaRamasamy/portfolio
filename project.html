AR-based laboratories: These allow students to conduct experiments and explore scientific concepts in a virtual setting. They can interact with virtual equipment, perform simulations, and observe realistic reactions, all without the need for physical lab resources.
Travel through History: AR can transport students to different historical periods or cultural contexts. By overlaying historical images, videos, or 3D models onto real-world locations, students can explore and interact with historical landmarks, artifacts, or events, fostering a deeper understanding of history and culture.
